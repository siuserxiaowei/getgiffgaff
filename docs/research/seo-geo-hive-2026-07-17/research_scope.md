# getgiffgaff SEO / GEO / earned-link research scope

## Research question

How can getgiffgaff improve organic-search discovery, AI-search citability, and ethical earned-link acquisition without changing the frozen 34-page legacy body/navigation/visual baseline or making unsupported commercial, carrier, payment, ranking, or compatibility claims?

## Boundary

- Geography: Chinese-speaking users in mainland China and people preparing to study, work, or travel in the United Kingdom.
- Languages: Simplified Chinese first; English sources for official carrier, regulator, search-engine, publisher, and technical evidence.
- Evidence date: 2026-07-17 (Asia/Shanghai).
- Timeframe: current public sources, with older evergreen sources retained only when their present applicability is verified.
- Audience: site owner, implementers, prospective editorial partners, and independent reviewers.
- Allowed: official documentation, public webpages, public search results, public repositories, public social/video search metadata, direct HTTP observations, and the repository's frozen/produced artifacts.
- Blocked: login-only/private data, paywalled text not supplied by the user, scraped personal contact data, bulk copyrighted text, link purchases, reciprocal-link schemes, fake reviews, fabricated placements, and claims inferred from search order.

## Source tiers

- Required: getgiffgaff production/repository, giffgaff help/terms/roaming pages, Ofcom, Google Search Central, Bing/IndexNow, OpenAI/Anthropic/Perplexity crawler documentation where applicable.
- Important: directly relevant publishers, university/student resources, telecom comparison sites, maintained public repositories, and public creator/video pages.
- Adjacent: public community discussions and social search results used only as language/problem signals.
- Blocked: Reddit and Xiaohongshu content requiring unavailable login state; paid SEO datasets without configured access; Baidu account-only diagnostics and OTP-gated verification.

## Stop conditions

- Thirty named research lanes and thirty execution/review lanes have distinct ledger rows. The research side consists of 29 worker lanes plus the R30 curator synthesis; the ledger does not prove that sixty independent agent processes ran or that all lanes were accepted.
- Required sources are verified, rejected, or explicitly marked blocked.
- Key recommendations use accepted evidence; single-source and conflicting findings are labeled.
- Repository changes preserve the legacy freeze and pass the available automated gates.
- External content is delivered as drafts and target-specific briefs; `contacted` and `live` remain empty until independently verified.

## Non-goals

- No promise of rankings, indexing, AI citations, payment success, OTP delivery, network coverage, permanent number retention, or official giffgaff status.
- No new indexable route without distinct intent, sufficient evidence, and explicit route-manifest approval.
- No contact, posting, PR creation, main-branch merge, or third-party account action in this research run.
