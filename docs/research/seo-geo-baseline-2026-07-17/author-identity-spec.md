Status: implementation specification only; no page or Schema change is made in this package.

## Visible identity

- `getgiffgaff 是独立第三方中文教程与销售服务站，不是 giffgaff Limited 官方网站、官方客服或授权代表。`
- Review date and a short description of the review method: compare each dynamic claim with the adjacent first-party source, record the date, and revise or withdraw the claim when the source changes.
- Correction channel: `siuserxy@gmail.com`.
- Revision history for material content changes.

## Role separation

- The author/editor role is responsible for page selection, source review and corrections.
- The customer-service role handles order communication only within the published credential boundary.
- A commerce widget mentioning 小玉 does not by itself establish page authorship or editorial review.

## Prohibited identity fields

Do not publish an identity-card name, home address, invented credential, invented employment history, `legalName`, `sameAs`, award, carrier authorization, official relationship or first-hand experience that cannot be retained and verified.

Do not add Person Schema merely because the role string exists. Person Schema is eligible for later review only after the same real, stable identity information is visibly present on the page or a stable author page and the owner has approved publication.

## Experience boundary

- Write “小玉实测” only when a dated, privacy-safe test record exists and matches the described device, location, SIM form and result.
- When no real experience record exists, write an official-source-based procedure and say that no first-hand result is being claimed.
- Never turn customer anecdotes, search snippets, reconstructed evidence cards or local tests into personal experience.

## Trademark boundary

The independent-third-party statement reduces ambiguity but is not evidence of authorization or trademark sufficiency. The domain contains the full `giffgaff` mark; external brand promotion, controlled `sameAs` expansion and claims about rights remain stopped pending qualified review.
