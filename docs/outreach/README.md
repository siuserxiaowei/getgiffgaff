# Backlink outreach tracker

This directory records researchable editorial opportunities for the five new indexable growth pages. It is a planning ledger rather than evidence of earned backlinks.

## Status meanings

- `research`: the page and audience are real but editorial fit or contact policy still needs checking.
- `qualified`: the page has a clear topical fit and a plausible editorial contact route. It does not mean anyone has been contacted or agreed to link.

`first_contact`, `next_follow_up`, and `live_url` intentionally remain empty until a real action or independently verifiable placement exists.

## Outreach rules

- Lead with the original checklist or tool that helps the prospect's readers.
- Disclose that getgiffgaff is an independent third-party tutorial and sales site.
- Do not buy links or offer reciprocal links.
- Do not post the same pitch in bulk or place promotional comments in old discussions.
- Do not claim a placement until its public URL has been checked manually.
- Remove a prospect if its contact policy rejects commercial resources.

The prospect URLs were selected from the existing six-source and forty-competitor research corpus. Their inclusion records topical relevance only; it is not an endorsement and does not imply traffic or ranking performance.
